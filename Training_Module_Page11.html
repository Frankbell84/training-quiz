Tile Roofing

  • Made of Clay, Concrete, Metal, or Composite – Known for strength, beauty, and longevity.
  • Available in styles – Barrel tile, Flat tile, and more.
  • Naturally insulated – Absorbs and releases heat slowly, helping reduce AC use and lower energy bills.
  • Longevity – Typically lasts 20–30 years. Some tile roofs last up to 100 years thanks to fire, rot, and insect resistance.
