Compensation Structure

  • Base Salary: $15/hr up to 40 hours per week (No Overtime)
  • Commissions:
    • Starts at 0.5% of contract amount, can increase to 1% with milestones:
    • $250k in net sales for the month: $500 Bonus
    • $500k in net sales: + $1,000 Bonus
    • $750k in net sales: All previous bonuses + 0.75% on all sales
    • $1M in net sales: $1,500 Bonus (from $250 & $500) + 1% commission on all sales
  • Confirmation Bonuses:
    • If you confirm someone else’s appointment and it sells, you earn a $50 bonus!
