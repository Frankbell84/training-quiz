Vinyl Windows
(CWS: Custom Window Systems)

Pros:

  • Energy Efficiency: Vinyl windows are excellent insulators. CWS windows include a third pane with Argon gas for superior insulation.
  • Sound Proofing: Standard Impact Glass provides 40% sound reduction. CWS Argon Impact Glass boosts that to 90%!

Cons:

  • Yellowing/Cracking: Common consumer concern. CWS uses Virgin Vinyl (not recycled) and offers a Lifetime Warranty to help address this.
  • Versatility: Vinyl cannot support oversized or thick glass. Not suitable for high-rise or high-pressure coastal zones.
  • Thick Frames: Vinyl’s lower strength requires thicker frames, which can limit design options (e.g., pocket sliders).
