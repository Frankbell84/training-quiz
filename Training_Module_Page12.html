Metal Roofing

  • Most expensive roofing option, but best cost/benefit ratio over time.
  • Available in a wide variety of colors.
  • Longevity: Metal roofs last 40–70+ years.
  • Fire Resistant, Impact Resistant, Ultra Durable.
  • Most energy efficient roof: reflects UV to keep attics cool and reduce A/C usage.
  • Maintenance free and 100% recyclable.
