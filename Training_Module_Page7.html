Different Window Types

Aluminum vs. Vinyl

Aluminum frames are stronger and longer-lasting, while Vinyl frames are more energy-efficient and corrosion-resistant. Both have their use cases, but aluminum is preferred for large windows and commercial applications.

Pros:

  • Versatility: Aluminum windows support stronger, thicker glass and larger sizes, making them ideal for both Residential and Commercial projects.
  • Longevity: Made of metal, aluminum windows typically outlast vinyl options.

Cons:

  • Corrosion/Pitting: Without protection, aluminum is vulnerable. Our ES Windows use Kynar Paint for superior resistance.
  • Energy Efficiency: Aluminum conducts heat more than vinyl, making it less effective at insulating homes.
