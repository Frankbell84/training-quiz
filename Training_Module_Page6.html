Window Appointment
Requirements:
  • Homeowner must have a genuine need or desire to replace their windows/doors or roof.
  • The project must meet our minimums:
    • For Windows/Doors, we use a “4 opening” minimum or the equivalent. For example:
      • 4 normal sized windows in separate openings or
      • 4 sets of single-entry doors (or 2 sets of sliding doors, French doors)
      • Combination of windows & doors. Count the “pieces”.
      • 1 Quadruple Slider (4 “pieces”) or 1 double door and 2 sidelights
  • All homeowners, anyone listed on the deed, spouses, decision-makers, family members, or anyone involved with the project MUST be present for the estimate.
    The only exception: when the project value is very high and the homeowners are genuinely never available together (e.g., pilot & doctor).
  • But why!?
    • Our lowest price – All parties present = no return visits, which helps keep costs low for the customer.
    • A happier customer – When all parties are present, there are no surprises! The project moves smoothly, and everyone is aware of what to expect throughout the project.
    • Lost in Translation – When a homeowner is missing from the estimate, major details are lost, and the estimate is relegated to, “Hey, the window guys were here… they said it would be $20,000.”
